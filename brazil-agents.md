## Legal & Compliance (Brazilian Market)

### Important Considerations:

1. **Gambling Regulation:**
   - Online gambling regulation still evolving in Brazil
   - Lottery specifically in grey area
   - Present as "global platform accessible to Brazilians"
   - Not marketed as "Brazilian lottery"

2. **Crypto Legal Status:**
   - Cryptocurrencies are legal in Brazil
   - Regulated by Central Bank (for exchanges)
   - USDT transactions are legal
   - Must declare crypto holdings on tax returns

3. **Age Verification:**
   - Must be 18+ (Brazilian law)
   - Clear statement in all materials
   - "Proibido para menores de 18 anos"

4. **Responsible Gaming:**
   - Include "Jogue com Responsabilidade"
   - Link to support resources

5. **Tax Implications:**
